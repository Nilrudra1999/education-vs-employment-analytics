Dataset to Database Conversion specifics
All data was gathered from Statistics Canada website Data section
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Dataset 14100287 Conversion specifics for tables
- based on Seasonally adjusted data 
- between ages 15 and over specifically
- limit the years to 2001 - 2024

Dataset 1410002301 group Conversion specifics
- tables will be created for each province:
- for each year and employment type 
- e.g. full-time, part-time, and unemployed
- industries will be made from North American Industry Classification System (NAICS)
- limit the industry names to:
    - Forestry, logging and support
    - Mining, quarrying, and oil and gas extraction
    - Utilities
    - Construction
    - Manufacturing
    - Trade
    - Transportation and warehousing
    - Information and cultural industries
    - Finance and insurance
    - Real estate and rental and leasing
    - Professional, scientific and technical services
    - Management of companies and enterprises
    - Administrative and support, waste management and remediation services
    - Educational services
    - Health care and social assistance
    - Arts, entertainment and recreation
    - Accommodation and food services
    - Public administration
- limit the years to 2001 - 2024

Dataset 1410020401 Conversion specifics
- limit the years to 2001 - 2024
- select NAICS categories
