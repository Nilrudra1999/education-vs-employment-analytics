Dataset to Database Conversion specifics
All data was gathered from the website of Statistics Canada - Data section
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Dataset 14100287 Conversion specifics
- based on Seasonally adjusted data only
- for ages 15 and over specifically
- limit the years to 2001 till 2024

Dataset group 1410002301 Conversion specifics
- tables will consolidate province, year and employment type data
    - e.g. full-time, part-time, and unemployed
- industries will be made from the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS)
    - Forestry, logging and support
    - Mining, quarrying, and oil and gas extraction
    - Utilities
    - Construction
    - Manufacturing
    - Trade
    - Transportation and warehousing
    - Information and cultural industries
    - Finance and insurance
    - Real estate and rental and leasing
    - Professional, scientific and technical services
    - Educational services
    - Health care and social assistance
    - Accommodation and food services
    - Public administration
- limit the years to 2001 till 2024

Dataset 1410020401 Conversion specifics
- limit the years to 2001 till 2024
- select NAICS categories



Tables
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- provinces

    - province_name

- employment_types
    - emp_typeID
    - emp_type_name

- industries

    - industry_name

- populations
    - province
    - amount
    - year

- workforce
    - employment_type
    - industry
    - number_of_people
    - year
    - province

- incomes
    - gross_amount
    - industry
